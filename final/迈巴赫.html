About the Mercedes-Benz Maybach S480

The Mercedes-Benz Maybach S480 is a luxury sedan that combines opulence with performance. It is equipped with a 3.0-liter inline-six turbocharged engine, delivering 367 horsepower and 500 Nm of torque. This powertrain enables the S480 to accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in approximately 5.8 seconds. The interior of the Maybach S480 is designed for maximum comfort and luxury, featuring high-quality materials and advanced technology. Passengers can enjoy features such as executive rear seats, ambient lighting, and a premium sound system. The vehicle also offers a suite of safety and driver assistance systems, ensuring a secure and comfortable driving experience. Originally introduced to the Chinese market, the Maybach S480 has garnered attention for its blend of performance and luxury. While availability may vary by region, it represents Mercedes-Benz's commitment to delivering high-end vehicles that cater to discerning customers seeking both elegance and performance.
