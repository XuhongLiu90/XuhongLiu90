About the BYD Qin L

The BYD Qin L is a stylish midsize sedan featuring BYD's latest DM-i hybrid system. It offers two EV ranges (80 km and 120 km) and a combined range of over 1,000 km, with fuel efficiency as low as 2.9 L/100 km. With a sleek design, high-tech interior, and advanced safety features like adaptive cruise control, the Qin L is a top choice for modern, eco-conscious drivers.
