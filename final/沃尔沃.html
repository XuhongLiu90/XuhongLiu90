About the Volvo S60

The Volvo S60 is a premium mid-size sedan blending Scandinavian design, advanced safety features, and efficient performance. It offers mild-hybrid and plug-in hybrid powertrains, with up to 455 horsepower in the Recharge T8. Inside, it boasts a minimalist, luxurious cabin with cutting-edge technology, including Google services and driver assistance systems like Pilot Assist. Stylish, safe, and versatile, the S60 is perfect for modern drivers seeking elegance and innovation.
