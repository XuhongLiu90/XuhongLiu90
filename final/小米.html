About the Xiaomi SU7

The Xiaomi SU7 is Xiaomi's first electric sedan, featuring sleek design, advanced technology, and strong performance. It offers single-motor and dual-motor configurations, with up to 673 PS and a range of 800 km. Equipped with a high-tech infotainment system and autonomous driving features, it’s priced competitively at around $30,000. The SU7 has quickly become a top-selling EV in China, positioning Xiaomi as a strong contender in the global EV market.
