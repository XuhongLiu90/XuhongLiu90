About the Tesla Model 3

The Tesla Model 3 is a fully electric mid-size sedan that combines performance, safety, and advanced technology. It offers quick acceleration, long-range capabilities, and access to Tesla's extensive Supercharger network. The minimalist interior features a central touchscreen controlling most functions, providing a modern driving experience. Recent updates have introduced touch-sensitive controls for indicators and gear selection, which have received mixed feedback.
