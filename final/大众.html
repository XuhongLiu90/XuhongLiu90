About the Volkswagen Tiguan

The Volkswagen Tiguan is a versatile compact SUV offering sleek design, a spacious interior for up to seven passengers, and advanced technology. Powered by a 2.0L turbo engine with 184 hp, it provides smooth performance and efficiency. With features like Apple CarPlay, adaptive cruise control, and lane-keeping assist, the Tiguan is a reliable and modern choice for families and adventurers alike.
