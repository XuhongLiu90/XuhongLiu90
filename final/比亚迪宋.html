About the BYD Song Plus DM

The BYD Song Plus DM-i is a compact SUV featuring BYD’s advanced DM-i hybrid system. It offers three EV ranges (75 km, 112 km, 160 km) with a combined range of up to 1500 km. Powered by a 1.5L engine and electric motor, it delivers efficient performance and modern features like a 15.6-inch touchscreen, adaptive cruise control, and smart safety systems. Stylish and versatile, it’s a strong contender in the hybrid SUV market.
