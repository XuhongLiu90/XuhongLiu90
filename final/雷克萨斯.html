About the Lexus ES

The Lexus ES is a midsize luxury sedan that seamlessly blends sophisticated design with advanced technology and exceptional comfort. Since its debut in 1989, the ES has evolved through multiple generations, consistently offering a refined driving experience. Design and Features The 2025 Lexus ES showcases a striking exterior with a distinctive spindle grille and sleek LED headlights. The interior is crafted with premium materials, providing a spacious and serene environment for both driver and passengers. Advanced infotainment systems and connectivity options ensure a modern driving experience. Performance The ES offers a range of powertrains to suit diverse preferences. The ES 350 is equipped with a 3.5-liter V6 engine, delivering robust performance. For those seeking enhanced efficiency, the ES 300h features a hybrid system that combines a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine with an electric motor, achieving impressive fuel economy. Additionally, the ES 250 variant provides all-wheel drive capability, enhancing traction and stability in various driving conditions. Safety Lexus prioritizes safety with the inclusion of the Lexus Safety System+ 2.5, which encompasses features such as Pre-Collision System with Pedestrian Detection, Lane Departure Alert with Steering Assist, and Dynamic Radar Cruise Control. These technologies work together to provide a secure driving environment. Availability The 2025 Lexus ES is available in various markets, including the United States and China. In China, the ES has been a popular choice among luxury sedan buyers, offering models like the ES 200 and ES 300h. For more detailed specifications and pricing, prospective buyers are encouraged to visit the official Lexus website or contact local dealerships.
